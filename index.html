Set 1

$ ls /gallery/set_1

My process focuses on emphasizing the hidden designs all around us. I enjoy being able to take an image from my camera and then, using contrast, color theory and software, manipulate it in a manner that deconstructs the original subject into basic parts that I can pick and choose from. This first set of mine features imagery of flowers, birds, ferns, lizards, and trees. While observing these entities, I enjoyed the smell and vibrancy of the flowers, the way the birds danced and taught their young, the drastic change of the ferns once it rained, the lizards who constantly chased each other and live (to this day) in the ac unit as if it were an apartment complex, and finally, the trees that began to sprout new growth and sway wonderfully in the wind.
